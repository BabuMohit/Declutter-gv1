Privacy Policy for Declutter!

Last updated: January 2024

Summary: Declutter! is designed with privacy as a core principle. All your data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to any external servers or third parties.

Introduction

Declutter! ("we", "our", or "the extension") is a Chrome extension that helps users manage browser tabs and capture full-page screenshots. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our extension.

Information We Collect

Declutter! collects and stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • Tab Information: URLs, titles, favicons, and tab states of your open browser tabs
  • Screenshots: Full-page screenshots you capture using the extension
  • Workspaces: Collections of tabs you save as workspaces
  • User Preferences: Settings and configurations you choose within the extension

How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely for the functionality of the extension:

  • To display and manage your browser tabs
  • To capture and display screenshots of web pages
  • To save and restore workspaces
  • To provide caching functionality for better performance

Data Storage and Transmission

All data collected by Declutter! is stored exclusively on your local device using:

  • IndexedDB (for screenshots and workspaces)
  • localStorage (for preferences and settings)

No data is ever transmitted to our servers, third-party services, or any external destinations. The extension operates entirely within your browser environment.

Permissions Explanation

Declutter! requires the following permissions for its functionality:

  • tabs: Used to manage and display your open tabs
  • activeTab: Used to interact with the currently active tab
  • scripting: Required to capture screenshots and inject capture script
  • storage & unlimitedStorage: Used to store tab screenshots and workspaces locally
  • tabGroups: Used to manage Chrome's native tab groups
  • host_permissions (all_urls): Required to capture screenshots across different websites

Data Retention and Deletion

Your data remains stored on your device until you clear it using the extension's clear cache function or uninstall the extension. You can delete all stored data at any time through the extension interface.

Children's Privacy

Declutter! does not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from children under 13. The extension provides the same privacy protections to all users regardless of age.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy.
