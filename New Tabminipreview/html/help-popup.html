Declutter! User Guide

Open Tabs Section

View and manage all your open browser tabs:

  • See thumbnails of all open tabs
  • Click "Sync List" to refresh tab list
  • Click "Load All Previews" to generate thumbnails

Capturing Screenshots

To capture a full-page screenshot:

  1. Navigate to the desired webpage
  2. Click on the tab in the Declutter! interface
  3. Wait for the screenshot to generate
  4. View or download the full-page screenshot

Saving Workspaces

Use the "Save" button to:

  • Preserve your current tab configuration
  • Create a snapshot of your browsing session
Pro Tip: Use "Clear Cache" to free up storage space and refresh thumbnails.
